Task Instruction

 

Overall Time: This task takes approximately 30-35 minutes. You will be compensated $0.50 for completing the hit.

 

First, in the Typing part of task, you will type 40 5-letter sequences presented in a random order, each 5 times.
You will practice overall 200 trials.

In the Evaluation part of task, you will be shown 40 pairs from the same sequences you just practiced.
Your task here will be to evaluate which sequence you typed faster and report your confidence about
your choice on the scale from 0 to 100. You will evaluate overall 200 pairs presented in 5 blocks of trials, 40 trials each.

 

 

IMPORTANT:

 

When you are typing the sequence the current letter will be colored green. When you type the correct letter, the next letter will turn green.
All of the letters are in lower-case. The green letter will not move forward unless the correct letter is typed. Make sure Caps Lock is turned OFF.

When you are evaluating sequences you will make your choice by clicking on it.

When you report your confidence the slider scale will be shown. You will drag and drop the slider pointer to a chosen value and then confirm it.
